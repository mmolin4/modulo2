Books

This photography project explores the intimate relationship between individuals and their books, capturing the emotions and stories that emerge through the act of reading. The images focus on personal spaces where literature is enjoyed, ranging from cozy home nooks to quiet library corners. Each photograph aims to convey the unique atmosphere of reading—whether through the calm of a moment deep in thought or the dynamic energy of imagination. The project highlights how books influence lives and inspire creativity, celebrating the timeless connection between readers and their stories.
